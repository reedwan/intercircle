Case study

Register with a GP

Register with a GP is a digital service which allows residents in London to find and register with a local GP by catchment area.

The NHS asked us to lead a detailed review and rebuild of an initial pilot. We redesigned the service to GDS standards, and rebuilt it on a modern application stack with careful consideration for the transmission and retention of official-sensitive data to standards set by the Cabinet Office. We used a cloud-first microservices approach, designed for scalability, high availability and security.

We now run the service for the NHS. It is used by over 180 GP practices and registers over 6,000 patients a month.
